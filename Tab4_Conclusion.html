Global Aviation: Emissions and Ownership

Every year, the aviation industry generates significant carbon emissions — with each country contributing differently based on its flight volume and fleet size. Beyond emissions, the number of aircraft owned by each country also shapes the global aviation network, influencing travel patterns, connectivity, and environmental impact.

This dashboard brings together two key indicators: Annual aviation-related CO₂ emissions and aircraft registration data by country.
